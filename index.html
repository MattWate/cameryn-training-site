Simulation-Based Learning and Enterprise Development Across Africa

Cameryn Training supports the design, facilitation, and implementation of simulation-based learning and enterprise development initiatives across Africa. The focus is on practical, decision-based learning approaches that help entrepreneurs, facilitators, and institutions move beyond theory into applied business practice.

  • Facilitation of entrepreneurship simulations (virtual and in-person)
  • Train-the-trainer and facilitator capacity-building programmes
  • Advisory support for enterprise development and donor-funded initiatives
